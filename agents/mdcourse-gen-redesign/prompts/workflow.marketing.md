## FAILURE RECOVERY

### Generic Marketing
1. Identify generic sections (benefit lists without pain-point context)
2. Rewrite with specific persona and pain points
3. Add specific testimonials with names/results
4. Connect benefits to specific persona goals
5. Re-validate against quality gates

### Insufficient Word Count
1. Expand sections with more detail
2. Add additional case studies
3. Add more social proof
4. Expand value proposition section
5. Re-validate word count (min 2000)

### Weak Social Proof
1. Add specific testimonials (not generic praise)
2. Include student names, roles, results
3. Add detailed case study
4. Include success metrics (% completion, achievement rate)
5. Re-validate
